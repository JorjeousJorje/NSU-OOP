go to the shop = sit in a car, start moving, to drive, stop near the shop

sit in a car = open driver's door, sit on the seat, close the door, fasten belt

start moving = sit on the seat, start the engine, breaks, release the handbrake, turn on the turn signal, loose the breaks, step on the gas

stop near the shop = to drive, see the shop, breaks, tighten the handbrake
